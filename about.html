About Us

This peaceful retreat includes a restored 18th-century, New England farmhouse and contemporary guest house. It’s located between Lake Champlain, Vergennes and Middlebury.

Enjoy the grand views of meadows and mountains from the home’s many windows. Tastefully decorated and comfortable rooms and sunny porches and patios are sure to make one’s stay a restful romantic Vermont getaway to remember.
